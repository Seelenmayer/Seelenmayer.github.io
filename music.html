    Music

    Music has always been a passion of mine. While I spent most of my life playing percussion, I’ve also explored many other fascinating instruments, including 6- and 12-string electric and acoustic guitars, 4- and 5-string basses, piano, xylophone, and even a pan flute!

    In this section, I’d like to share a few of my recordings. First, Diablo’s Tristram town theme on guitar, followed by MapleStory’s Ellinia town theme on piano, and finally, MapleStory’s Orbis town theme on xylophone.
